Contact

Contact pages help your visitors reach you easily be sending you direct emails. Deliver provide you with active contact forms and google maps to make the best use of your contact page.

Conact Form

A simple contact form with beautifully designed inputs that will hep your visitor reach you. Any contact form consists of email, name and textarea inputs. Take a look at its simple mark up. After the visitor write his message and hit submit button, he will be redirected to a conifrm page that tell him that he sent the message successfully.

Example


<form action="contactform/contact-form-handler.php" method="post" name="contactform">
    <div class="col-md-6 left-side">
        <input type="text" name="name" id="name" class="form-control fix" placeholder="Full Name">
        <input type="tel" class="form-control fix" placeholder="Phone">
    </div>
    <div class="col-md-6 right-side">
        <input type="email" name="email" id="email" class="form-control fix-bottom" placeholder="Email Address">
        <input type="text" class="form-control fix-bottom" placeholder="Website">
    </div>
    <div class="col-xs-12">
        <textarea rows="10" class="form-control" name="message" id="message" placeholder="Message Here"></textarea>
        <div class="text-center">
            <button type="submit" name="submit" class="btn btn-default btn-fullwidth btn-dark">Send Message</button>
        </div>
    </div>
</form>

To recieve the messages to your email, you should change it to yours from the contact form the email handler page and here is how:

  • 1. Open the HTML folder.
  • 2. Open the contact form folder.
  • 3. Open the 'contact-form-handler.php' file.
  • 4. Go to line 8 and change the email to yours.
