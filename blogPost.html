Caribbean Island

Affordable Style

One of the best parts about the Caribbean is how chill it is. In fact, it is pretty much the opposite of New York, where I live :). My boyfriend and I – in need of relaxation, sun, and warm – traveled to a couple islands. The three islands we traveled to were: St. Maarten for Christmas (more pics/activities here: 6 reasons to visit and 5 affordable things to do), St. Lucia for my birthday, and Cancun over Easter

Something about the fact that there’s minimal fabric means that the prices should also be minimal. Haha! Seriously – much of my warm weather clothing has been thrifted, repurposed, or purchased at extremely low prices. I’m sharing some of my inexpensive Caribbean outfits, that also reflect the colorful, relaxed vibes of the islands, mon! Shopping info is included….

The nautical striped shorts are by Joe Fresh, but I got them from Buffalo Exchange. The sandals are by Paul Andrew, I rave about them because I snagged them for the low-low at a sample sale (some of my best sample sale finds are here). Glasses are by Perverse. This top is from Hustle Vintage, the shorts are from Target, and the sandals are Via Spiga which I got from Nordstrom Rack.
